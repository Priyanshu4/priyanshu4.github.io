About Me

Get to know me!

Hey! It's Priyanshu Agrawal and I'm a student at the University of Connecticut studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. My coursework is concentrated in machine learning as well as signal processing and control systems. I'm passionate about developing software for intelligent physical systems, such as robots and autonomous vehicles. I'm currently researching motion planning algorithms for robotic manipulators used in manufacturing robots. In my past internships and research experiences, I've developed software for embedded Linux sytems, a communication system for an autonomous rover, machine learning algorithms for classification of cell types and more. In this portfolio, I've highlighted some of my best projects outside of my coursework, research and internships.

Feel free to contact me at priyanshu.agrawal@uconn.edu.
